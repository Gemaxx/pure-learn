# Epics List

## 1. Process & Structure

1. **User Onboarding**
   - Let user define himself, like his goals, education, job and so on. Why he wanna use this app (select from suggestions reasons or he can add). Giving Quick App Tut.
2. **Goal Management**
   - Allow users to set, adjust, and track their learningCategories (Categories is a group of goals, a name for area that he wanna work on like Self -Improvement, Software Engineering, etc.), goals, and tasks, learning resources, notes need to achieve this goals.
3. **Time Management**
   - Provide tools for users to plan & schedule learning activities and set reminders to stay on track. Timers, focus sessions and so on.
4. **Progress Tracking, Reviewing & Reporting**
   - Offer insights and analytics on learning progress, achievements, and areas for improvement.

## 2. Problems & Challenges

1. **Distraction Management**
   - Help users identify and mitigate distractions, enabling them to focus better on their study sessions. Tracking time spent on applications, blocking apps & websites or putting time limits for it.
2. **Motivation & Accountability Tools**
   - Provide resources and reminders that encourage consistency and build productive study habits. Ways to increase Accountabilities.Like sharing daily tasks & daily achievements.
3. **Learning Pace Adjustment**
   - Offer options for adjusting study pace based on progress and personal needs, supporting flexibility in the learning process.

## 3. Best Practices & Theories

1. **Productivity Tips & Techniques**
   - Integrate best practices for time management, focus strategies, and energy optimization.
2. **Reflection & Self -Assessment**
   - Encourage users to reflect on their learning, assessing strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.
3. **Theory -Based Learning Resources**
   - Provide in -app content on learning theories and best practices to help users understand and apply effective learning process and deep work.

## 4. User Account Management

1. **Authentication & Authorization**
   - Manages user login, signup, password recovery, and security settings (2FA, password reset).
2. **User Profile Management**
   - Allows users to manage personal profile, upload photos, and set/update learning preferences. Exporting cv and sharing achievements.
3. **Account Settings & Deactivation**
   - Enables users to modify account settings, deactivate accounts, and confirms deactivation via email.
